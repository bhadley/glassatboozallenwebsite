Diagram Based Information Entry

Title
Select Trigger





Many of the world's questions can be answered with either a yes or a no. By answering yes/no questions, you can provide your users with the information relevant to them. This page allows users to input instruction sets to Glass that resemble yes/no decision trees

The tree you create must be a binary tree, meaning each node can only have one parent, and each parent can only have two children.

To add children to a node, hover over the node until you see a '+' in the upper right corner. You can then click the plus, and the children will appear. Double click the text inside a node to edit it. This is the text Glass will display on the screen.

You can delete a node by clicking it and pressing the delete key on your keyboard. You can undo and redo actions in the diagram by using ctrl+z and ctrl+y.
